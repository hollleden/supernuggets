# get supernuggets on your home screen

okay quick — 30 seconds, one time, then it's just an app.

## iPhone (Safari only, sorry)
1. open supernuggets.app in Safari
2. tap Share ↑
3. "Add to Home Screen" → Add

## Android (Chrome)
1. open supernuggets.app in Chrome
2. tap ⋮ → "Add to Home screen" → Add

done. full-screen, real icon, no browser bar.
